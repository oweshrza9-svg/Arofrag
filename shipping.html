Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about delivery and returns.

Shipping Policy

Orders are processed within 24 hours after confirmation.

Estimated delivery time is 3–7 business days depending on your location.

You will receive a tracking number once your order has been shipped.

Return Policy

Returns are accepted only for damaged, defective or incorrect products.

The return request must be submitted within 7 days of delivery.

Refund Policy

Approved refunds are processed within 5–7 business days through the original payment method.

Need Help?

If you have any questions regarding shipping or returns, please contact our support team.
