## 📖 About

**AROFRAG** is a premium online fragrance platform specializing in **alcohol-free handcrafted attars** inspired by the rich heritage of Indian perfumery.

The goal of this project is to provide a seamless online shopping experience while preserving the authenticity of traditional attars through a modern and elegant digital interface.
